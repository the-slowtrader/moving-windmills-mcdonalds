William's Story — What Happened Next

Young William / Malawi landscape

The book tells the story of William Kamkwamba, who built a windmill from scrap parts and a library book.

His lights came on. His community changed.

That part is true. All of it.

William at TED / Dartmouth / book

William's story traveled.

A TED talk. A bestselling book. A Netflix film. He studied in the U.S. and at African Leadership Academy.

But then he came back.

William in Kasungu — present day

William returned to Wimbe — the same community where he built the windmill — and started building something bigger.

Community hub — wide shot

Today, Moving Windmills Project runs a network of community hubs across a 40-kilometer radius — places where young people learn engineering, solar technology, sustainable farming, and how to solve problems in their own community.

They share meals. They teach each other. People just show up.
